Hi Drew - I'm very pleased! I was holding 30 mph on the flat into a head wind yesterday - they're bloody fantastic! Hugely stiffer than the 303's they replace - you don't realise how flexi Zipp are til you replace them. And all my evidence is empirical, backed up by power. I realise Spanish beef aside I will never be a great climber, but these dividends make a massive difference to mental health being a 'rouleur' style of rider!
