Spin On These Fast Carbon Wheels / ...because life's too short for slow boring bikes, uncomfortable rides and zero grip. Spin on these Fat Boys instead and upgrade your ride to effortless speed, gut wrenching adhesion in all weathers and truly sublime comfort over any type of surface. But there's more to the Fat Boys and the people who make 'em. You see, every wheel we make is precision hand crafted in the UK specifically for just one rider - that's You! What that means is a wheel uniquely engineered with your requirements in mind to deliver the ride of your life. Then there's our Maker to Rider Direct pricing which means you're never paying extra for a bunch of other dudes to sit in the middle. And it means whenever you need help or advice - before or after purchase - who better to ask than the people who actually made your wheels. If you've never heard of Spin On These Wheels before, scroll down this page to find riders just like you who're already spinning up their Fat Boys and enjoying the ride like never before. One day we hope to help transform your ride too - thanks for riding by!
