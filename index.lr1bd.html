Fab wheels!

Sean on the DM845 Fat Boys he just slotted in

Sean Macconnamara / The Speed Shop
Drew, Fab wheels! Just spent a week riding these fat boys around the mountains of Gran Canaria shod with Vittoria Corsa g+ tyres 23c. Very responsive up the climbs and extremely stiff. Descending with these wheels gives you masses of confidence, bank them over more than you ever dared before and the grip is amazing. The Ceramic Braking surface combined with the brake blocks provided gives fantastic stopping performance, great modulation also. Last but not least , they look damn "COOL". Thanks Drew!
