The Spin Meister

I’ve engineered these Koppenberg DM8 Super Fat Boys to be aero, light, stiff, robust, durable …and all that other stuff every brand of wheels claims.

But with the DM8 series rim profiles, I’ve also aimed for something significantly better than any other high-performance wheel on the market is able to deliver. That is, optimise the ride performance of 25mm (and bigger) tyres.

At the end of the day, the entire purpose of your wheels (in fact, the only purpose of your entire bike!) is to connect you to the planet - as fast and securely and with as much feedback and fun as possible. And to do this well, it simply all boils down to how your tyre is served and delivered up to the road, the trail or the boards. So the shape of the tyre tread, and how this influences its performance is something I’ve spent a great deal of time investigating. And it’s with this aim of creating a perfectly arced tread profile, from one rim edge to the other that the DM8 series is born.

What this means to you out on the bike is more speed, more grip, more comfort, and more safety. And all of this with less effort.

DM8s are upto 30mm at their widest, yet fit most modern road, race, track, gravel and cx bikes thanks to a tapered edge design that narrows down to under 27mm. Choose from two build variations and three rim depths, and from 1470 grams and £990 / pair.

Every DM8 wheel is built entirely by hand, by me, and customised for you and the way you ride. They’re the best wheels I’ve ever made, and I’m confident that they’re also the best wheels you’ll have experienced, ever.
